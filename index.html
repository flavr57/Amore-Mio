NHR TAX REGIME
Portugal's updated IFICI tax incentive — the successor to the original NHR regime — offers a flat 20% income tax rate on Portuguese-sourced income and favorable treatment of certain foreign income for qualifying new residents for a 10-year period. Those who registered under the original NHR before its 2024 sunset continue to enjoy their existing benefits in full.
