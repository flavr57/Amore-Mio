COST OF LIVING
Lisbon and Porto have seen continued upward pressure on everyday costs, with groceries and dining roughly 20–30% above the national average, though both cities remain meaningfully more affordable than comparable Western European capitals. NHR TAX REGIME
Portugal's replacement for NHR — the IFICI regime (also called NHR 2.0) — offers a flat 20% tax rate on Portuguese-source income and potential exemptions on foreign income for qualifying new residents for a 10-year period. Expats with eligible professions or passive income should consult a Portuguese fiscal advisor early, as the application window opens upon establishing residency.

Property prices in Lisbon and the Algarve remain elevated, with demand from international buyers keeping the market competitive well into 2026. World-First Conservation Win Restores Peace to Ireland's Greatest Seabird Colony
In a genuine world first, conservationists have successfully eradicated an invasive population of ferrets from Rathlin Island off Northern Ireland — home to the country's largest seabird colony and thousands of nesting puffins, razorbills, and guillemots. The painstaking effort required years of trapping, monitoring, and collaboration between wildlife agencies and local communities, and the results are already visible in healthier, less-disturbed nesting seasons. It is a reminder that some of the most meaningful environmental victories happen quietly, on a wind-swept island, one careful step at a time.

On April 25, 1954, Bell Labs unveiled the first functional silicon solar cell — a modest panel of metal and glass that could convert sunlight into electricity, quietly setting the stage for one of the most transformative energy revolutions in human history. What began as a laboratory curiosity now powers homes, satellites, and entire cities across the planet. Give Your Bee Hotel a Spring Check-Up This Weekend
If you have a bee hotel in your garden — or have been thinking about adding one — this weekend is the perfect moment to give it a careful once-over, as solitary bees like red mason and leafcutter bees are actively searching for nesting sites right now. Clear out any debris or parasitized tubes from last season, ensure the structure is tilted slightly downward to shed rain, and position it in a sunny, sheltered spot facing south or southeast for best results. Even a small bee hotel is an extraordinary act of hospitality: you are offering a home to one of the garden's most diligent and irreplaceable guests.

Plant Summer Bulbs Now for a Garden That Truly Glows
Late April is prime time to get summer-flowering bulbs in the ground — dahlias, gladioli, begonias, and freesias all want to be planted now so they can establish roots before the warmth of early summer coaxes them skyward. In coastal California's mild spring, you have a gentle window to work with: loosen the soil well, add a little grit for drainage, and set each bulb at the depth recommended on the packet — usually two to three times the bulb's own height. The small act of pressing a bulb into the earth carries an almost irrational hopefulness, which is precisely why gardeners keep doing it.

The Science of Why Your Dog Always Knows When You Need a Hug
Research in animal cognition has consistently shown that dogs are uniquely attuned to human emotional states — able to read facial expressions, body language, and even the biochemical signatures of stress in ways that no other domesticated species can quite match. It is, when you sit with it, one of the most extraordinary facts about sharing your life with a dog: they have been paying close attention to you, all along.
